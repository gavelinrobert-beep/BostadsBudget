# Privacy Policy for Bostadsbudget

**Last Updated: February 2026**

## Introduction

Bostadsbudget ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Calculator Input Data**: Housing prices, down payments, interest rates, maintenance costs, and renovation budgets that you enter into the calculator
- This data is stored locally on your device only

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App features used, calculation frequency (if analytics are enabled)
- **Technical Data**: Crash reports, performance data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain our calculator service
- Calculate housing costs based on your input
- Improve and optimize the app experience
- Fix bugs and improve performance
- Respond to user inquiries and support requests

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- All calculator input data is stored **locally on your device only**
- We use AsyncStorage (mobile) and localStorage (web) for data persistence
- Your financial calculations are **never transmitted to our servers**
- You can clear this data at any time by clearing app data or browser cache

### Cloud Services
- We do not store your personal or financial data on cloud servers
- Anonymous usage statistics may be collected through third-party analytics (if enabled)

## Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect information:

### Analytics (Optional)
- **Google Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics
- **Vercel Analytics**: Anonymous performance metrics (web app)
- **Expo Analytics**: Anonymous crash reports and usage data (mobile app)

These services do not have access to your calculator input data.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:
- Local data storage with device-level encryption
- HTTPS encryption for web traffic
- Regular security updates and patches
- No transmission of sensitive financial data

## Your Data Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View what data is stored on your device
- **Delete**: Clear all locally stored data through app settings or browser cache
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics tracking if implemented
- **Export**: Save your calculations locally

### How to Exercise Your Rights
- **Mobile App**: Clear app data in device settings
- **Web App**: Clear browser cache and cookies
- **Contact Us**: Email support@bostadsbudget.com for assistance

## Children's Privacy

Bostadsbudget is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## International Users

Bostadsbudget is designed for Swedish users but may be accessed internationally. Your data remains on your local device regardless of your location.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Displaying a notice in the app/website
- Sending an email notification (if we have your email)

Continued use of Bostadsbudget after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

## GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have additional rights:

### Legal Basis for Processing
- **Consent**: You provide consent by using the app
- **Legitimate Interest**: We have a legitimate interest in improving our service

### Your GDPR Rights
- Right to access your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Rights related to automated decision-making

### Data Retention
- Calculator data: Stored locally until you delete it
- Analytics data: Retained for up to 26 months (Google Analytics)
- Crash reports: Retained for up to 90 days
